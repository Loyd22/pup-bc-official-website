Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

(BSIT)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program equips students to design, build, and secure digital solutions that help organizations operate efficiently and responsibly.

Job Opportunities

Graduates of the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program can pursue various career paths in the technology industry:

  • Technical Support Specialist
  • Computer Operator
  • Network Engineer
  • Network Administrator
  • Computer Programmer
  • Software Engineer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Systems Developer
  • Web Designer
  • Database Administrator
  • Researcher
  • Information Technology Project Manager
  • Information Technology Instructor
  • Information Technology Entrepreneur
  • Information Security Administrator
