Diploma in Computer Engineering Technology

(DCPET)

The Diploma in Computer Engineering Technology provides immersive training in electronics, automation, and networked devices to support intelligent systems in industry.

Job Opportunities

Graduates of the Diploma in Computer Engineering Technology program can pursue various career paths based on their year level:

1st Year

  • Computer Repair Shop Assistant
  • Computer Technician
  • Computer Operator
  • System Support Technician
  • Junior Computer Service Technician

2nd Year

  • Senior Computer Service Technician
  • Computer Peripheral Technician

3rd Year

  • Computer Engineer Assistant
  • Computer System Designer Assistant
  • Senior Programmer
  • Web Designer / Developer
  • Web Administrator
  • System Administrator
  • Computer Trainer
  • System Researcher
  • Technical Support
  • Supervisor / Manager
